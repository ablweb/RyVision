About This Website

This is a static, open-source website showcasing AI-generated mini-games. The project is hosted on GitHub and deployed with Vercel. Every few days, an autonomous AI agent creates a completely new game with no human involvement.
We do not collect or store personal data. Only third-party services such as Google AdSense may use cookies. No additional data is required to play or access this site.
AI is rapidly transforming entertainment, and AI-generated video games represent the next frontier.
